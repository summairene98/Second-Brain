## Irene's 6 Core Response Strategies

1. **Acknowledge + Validate** — "Capisco perfettamente - io stessa..."
2. **Reframe as Feature** — Turn objection into reason FOR the course
3. **Offer Concrete Flexibility** — Payment plans, pause options, async format
4. **Social Proof** — "Ho farmacisti come te che..."
5. **Cost of Inaction** — "Se non fai niente, tra 6 mesi..."
6. **Time Scarcity** — "Chiudo le iscrizioni fine giugno"

**Most effective:** Reframe + Flexibility (70% of objections)

## New Pattern Highlights

### ✅ **Lack of Autonomy** (3 files)
Not just low pay — feeling micromanaged, controlled in decisions.
- **Response:** Emphasize complete autonomy online

### ✅ **Privacy/Family Concerns** (2 files)
Fear of children/family being exposed, not general social media anxiety.
- **Response:** Assure anonymity options, professional persona only

### ✅ **Workplace Toxicity** (2 files)
Colleague conflicts, jealousy, gossip — not just management stress.
- **Response:** Online removes peer toxicity, mindset work helps heal trauma

### ✅ **Legal/VAT Specifics** (2 files)
Detailed questions about €5K threshold, Partita IVA, tax obligations.
- **Response:** Reference expert guidance, commercialista support

### ✅ **Personal Life Crisis** (2 files)
Family emergency, caregiving, illness — cannot commit right now.
- **Response:** Offer pause/resume flexibility, build goodwill for later
